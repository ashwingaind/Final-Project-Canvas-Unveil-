Moe Gallery

In its owner’s words, Moe is not like one of those high end galleries where you might feel like you don’t belong. The vision is to create a gallery with a special space and a platform for modern and contemporary art. a platform where artists and art enthusiasts can exchange knowledge, ideas and experiences. Their goal is to create a meaningful dialogue between artists and audience.
